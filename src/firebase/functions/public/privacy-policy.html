Welcome to WordWar! This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our mobile application and related services.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

  • Account Information: When you sign in with Google, we collect your Google account email address and display name.
  • Game Data: We collect information about your gameplay, including game scores, statistics, and performance metrics.
  • User Profile: Any additional information you choose to provide in your user profile.

1.2 Automatically Collected Information

  • Device Information: Device type, operating system version, and unique device identifiers.
  • Usage Data: How you interact with the app, features used, and time spent in the app.
  • Game Analytics: Game performance, match results, and player interactions.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • Game Functionality: To provide and maintain the multiplayer word game experience.
  • User Experience: To personalize your gaming experience and improve our services.
  • Matchmaking: To connect you with other players for multiplayer games.
  • Analytics: To analyze game performance and improve our services.
  • Support: To provide customer support and respond to your inquiries.

3. Information Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to third parties, except in the following circumstances:

  • With Your Consent: When you explicitly agree to share your information.
  • Service Providers: With trusted third-party services that help us operate our app (e.g., Firebase for authentication and data storage).
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law or to protect our rights and safety.
  • Game Features: Your username and game statistics may be visible to other players in multiplayer games.

4. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal information:

  • Encryption of data in transit and at rest
  • Secure authentication through Google Sign-In
  • Regular security assessments and updates
  • Limited access to personal information on a need-to-know basis

5. Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as necessary to:

  • Provide our services
  • Comply with legal obligations
  • Resolve disputes
  • Enforce our agreements

You can request deletion of your account and associated data at any time through the app settings.

6. Your Rights and Choices

You have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • Access: Request access to your personal information.
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate information.
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your account and data.
  • Opt-out: Opt out of certain data collection and processing.

Account Deletion: You can delete your account at any time through the app settings or by visiting our Account Deletion Page. For assistance with account deletion, please contact our support team.

7. Third-Party Services

Our app uses the following third-party services:

  • Google Sign-In: For user authentication (subject to Google's Privacy Policy)
  • Firebase: For backend services, analytics, and data storage (subject to Google's Privacy Policy)

8. Children's Privacy

WordWar is designed to be family-friendly and can be enjoyed by users of all ages, including children. We are committed to protecting the privacy of all our users, especially children.

For users under 13 years of age, we require parental consent before collecting any personal information. Parents can provide consent through our app or by contacting us directly.

If you are a parent or guardian and would like to review, delete, or refuse further collection of your child's information, please contact us immediately. We take children's privacy very seriously and will respond to all parental requests promptly.

All content in WordWar is designed to be appropriate for children and families. We maintain strict content standards and do not include any material that could be harmful, inappropriate, or unsuitable for young users.

All APIs, SDKs, and third-party services used in WordWar comply with applicable laws and regulations relating to children, including:
