Frequently Asked Questions

How do I start playing WordWar?
Download the app from the Google Play Store, sign in with your Google account, and you're ready to play! You can start with a tutorial or jump straight into multiplayer matches.
How does the multiplayer system work?
WordWar uses real-time matchmaking to connect you with other players. You can create private games with friends or join public matches. The system automatically finds opponents of similar skill levels.
What happens if I lose connection during a game?
If you lose connection, the game will attempt to reconnect you automatically. If reconnection fails, the game will be paused and you can resume when you're back online. Extended disconnections may result in a forfeit.
How do I improve my score and ranking?
Practice regularly, learn new words, and develop strategic thinking. Longer words score more points, and bonus tiles can significantly boost your score. Study your opponents' strategies and learn from each game.
Can I play offline?
WordWar requires an internet connection for multiplayer features. However, you can practice with the tutorial and review your game statistics offline.
How do I report inappropriate behavior?
If you encounter inappropriate behavior from other players, use the report feature in the game or contact our support team directly. We take all reports seriously and will investigate promptly.
What are the system requirements?
WordWar requires Android 7.0 (API level 24) or higher, at least 100MB of free storage space, and a stable internet connection for the best experience.

Game Rules & Tips

Basic Rules

  • Players take turns placing letters on the game grid
  • Words must be at least 3 letters long
  • Words can be formed horizontally, vertically, or diagonally
  • Bonus tiles multiply your word score
  • The player with the highest score at the end wins

Pro Tips

  • Look for opportunities to create multiple words in one move
  • Use bonus tiles strategically for maximum points
  • Plan your moves ahead to get more score than opponents
  • Learn common word patterns and prefixes/suffixes
  • Practice regularly to improve your vocabulary

Families Policy & Child Safety

WordWar is committed to providing a safe, family-friendly gaming experience that complies with Google Play's Families Policy.

Is WordWar safe for children?
Yes! WordWar is designed to be completely safe for children of all ages. All content is family-friendly, educational, and appropriate for young users. We maintain strict content standards and do not include any harmful or inappropriate material.
What about advertisements?
We only display advertisements that are appropriate for children and families. All ads come from Google Play certified ad networks or from our own trusted brand partnerships. We never show inappropriate, violent, or harmful advertisements to any users.
How do you protect children's privacy?
We comply with all applicable children's privacy laws including COPPA and GDPR. Users under 13 require parental consent, and parents have full control over their child's data. We never collect more information than necessary and always prioritize children's safety.
What content moderation do you have?
We implement comprehensive content moderation systems that automatically filter inappropriate content. All usernames, profile information, and user-generated content must be family-friendly. Users can report any concerning content for immediate review.
