Families Policy & Child Safety

WordWar is committed to providing a safe, family-friendly gaming experience that complies with Google Play's Families Policy. If you have concerns about child safety or content appropriateness, please contact us immediately.

Our commitment to families:

  • Child-Appropriate Content: All content is designed to be safe for users of all ages
  • Family-Friendly Advertising: We only display ads that are appropriate for children and families
  • Privacy Protection: We comply with COPPA, GDPR, and other children's privacy laws
  • Content Moderation: Robust systems ensure all user-generated content remains appropriate
  • Parental Control: Parents have full control over their child's data and account

For parents and guardians: If you have concerns about your child's experience with WordWar, need to review or delete your child's data, or want to report inappropriate content, please contact us immediately. We take all family safety concerns very seriously and will respond promptly.
